Philisa Abafazi Bethu - Cape Town Study Abroad

This past summer I raised money in efforts to go abroad to South Africa. While abroad I was able to take classes at The University of Cape Town as well as intern within the townships with Philisa Abafazi Bethu, a battered women and children's shelter. I was able to use my creative talents to paint a mural on their entry wall, inclusive fof the children's handprints as well as setup a bakery business model to create sustainable income for the shelter.
